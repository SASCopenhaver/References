Requesting Access page

The Requesting Access page displays the registration data for persons who have registered for the Alumni Archive website, but have not yet been verified and granted access.

If you are a PAO or Domestic Office Director, you will see a list of the people who have registered and need to be verified/approved by you before they can be granted access to the website.

You can click on one of the three option buttons on the right to Approve or Deny access, or you can put their registration On Hold until more information is obtained.
