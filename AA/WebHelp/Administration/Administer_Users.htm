Administer Users

What appears on the Administer Users page depends on your assigned role for the Alumni Archives. The page lists and briefly describes pages that are used to administer users and registrations. Links to these pages are provided by the navigation buttons in the left column.

If you do not have any administrative duties for the Alumni Archive, you can click on the [xxxxxxxxxx] link to open the User Profile page to update your own registration data.

Please keep your registration data up-to-date. Any changes in contact information, assignments, names, or roles, should be entered as soon as possible.

For persons with some administrative responsibilities:

  • If you are a PAO or Chief American PD Officer (stationed abroad), or a Domestic Office Director, then you are the Alumni Archive authorizing agent for any users at your post or in your office.

  • There are also Alumni Archive site administrators, with responsibilities for all users.

  • In these roles, you can access the user records that you need to administer, starting from the four user category pages. These pages can be used to change users' access statuses. They can also be used to open the User Profile page, where you can update the registration data for a user, or change their access status.

For more information on each of these pages, see:

  • Users Requesting Access to the website (users with pending requests).

  • Users Requesting Changes in their access.

  • Active Users (those who are already registered).

  • Pre-Registered Users (those who, because their positions make them authorizing agents, have been registered by the website administrators, without having requested access).

Also, a Quick Search tool is available for finding specific users.
