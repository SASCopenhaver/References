Approve Changes page

The Approve Changes page lists any users from your area who have requested certain changes in their registration. These include changes in their Role, Country or City of Assignment, or Additional Countries.

The list also includes any other users whose current status is On Hold.

Please process these users as soon as possible, because users cannot access the Archive while On Hold.

You can change their Status here, by clicking on the Approve or Deny option buttons, on the right.

The Deny button will drop the user from the Archive users list, and they will no longer have access the Archive. To regain access, they would have to re-register and be approved.

The Approve button gives the user access to the Alumni Archive, at the level dictated by their updated registration data.

Double click on a person's name to open the User Profile page, to display their full registration data.

If you have approved a user but now wish to deny them access, go to the See Your Active Users page. There you can change any active user's status.
