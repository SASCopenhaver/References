Pre-Registered Users

The Pre-registered Users page displays a list of the users who have been pre-registered by the Alumni Archive Administrator. These persons have been pre-registered because, by virtue of their roles in DOS, they are automatically the authorizing agents for a set of potential users. For example, PAOs are the authorizing agents for all other users in their country.

Click on a name to open a User Profile page displaying the registration data for that person.

Click on an option button to Deny or Approve access for one of these persons.
