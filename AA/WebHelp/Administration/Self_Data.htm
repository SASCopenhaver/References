Self Data

The Self Data page is used to update your own registration data, and to change your password.

The form displayed here is the same as the one used when you registered.

  1. Update any of your data that has changed.

    • Please be sure that all contact data is correct.

    • If you change your Role, the appropriate field controls will be displayed, to allow you to enter the necessary data.

  2. Change your password, if you have had it for more than 150 days (the DOS maximum is 180 days).

  3. Click on the Submit button.

Note that if you change your role, your country or city of assignment, or your departure date, or if you add any additional countries, your access will be put on hold temporarily, just until the authorizing agent can validate the change. This is to ensure that you will be given the appropriate access that is needed for your new assignment or role.
