Administration for PAOs and Office Directors

If you are a PAO or Chief American PD Officer (stationed abroad), or a DoS Domestic Office Director, then you are the Alumni Archive authorizing agent for any potential users from your Post or Country, or from your Office.

  • This means that you are the Officer responsible for granting or denying access to the Archive, for anyone from your area who has requested access by registering on the website.  "Your area" means your Post abroad (the city, country, or countries for which you function as the principal PD Officer), or your DOS domestic Office.

  • Normally, access is granted to any American Officers, American contractors, or FSNs who are in a position to update the Archive's alumni records, or who need access to the Archive in order to generate alumni reports, contact lists or mailing labels. This usually includes all PD personnel, and may include others.

  • Verification is needed when a DOS employee initially requests access, and when a current user moves to a new assignment -- a new Post or Office, or a new Role.

Therefore, you see a slightly different version of the Home page and the Admin page than do most users. The go here to approve your users link, at the bottom of the Admin block on the Home page, will take you directly to the Verify and Change Users page.

And on the Verify and Change Users page you have links to the Approve Access, Approve Changes, and See Your Active Users pages, all displayed in the left panel.

You should receive an email alerting you when a registrant from your area has submitted a requested access, or a current user has submitted changes that need you approval.

Please process these requests as soon as possible.
 

To process requests for new access from DOS employees in your area:

  1. Open the Approve Access page by clicking on the link on the left.

  2. That page will display the names of any DOS employees from your area who have registered and are awaiting your authorization. It will also list any additional countries for which they are requesting access.

  3. For each registrant, confirm whether their application for access is appropriate.

  4. You can click on one of the three option buttons to the right to Approve or Deny access, or you can put their registration On Hold until more information is obtained.

    • Clicking on the Deny button will drop the user from the Archive registrants list. They can apply again.

    • Clicking on the On Hold button will transfer the person to your list on the Approve Changes page, where their status can be finalized by using the Deny or Approve buttons.
       

To process requests for changes from DOS employees in your area:

  1. Open the Approve Changes page by clicking on the link on the left of the Verify and Changes Users page.

    •  The users listed here have already had their status changed to On Hold, and cannot access the Archive until you process their changes. This includes users who have made changes in their Role, Country or City of Assignment, or Additional Countries.  It also includes any other users whose status you have changed to On Hold.

  2. Confirm that their changes are appropriate and correct.

  3. You can click on either the Approve or Deny option button to the right.

    • Clicking on the Deny button will drop the user from the Archive users list, and they will no longer have access the Archive. To regain access, they would need to re-register and be approved.
       

Active Users

To see a list of all active users in your area, open the See Your Active Users page by clicking on the link on the left.

  • This page can be used if, for example, you need to remove or suspend access for a user. Use the Deny or On Hold radio buttons, as appropriate.

  • You can also click on the name of a user to open their User Profile page. There, you can update the registration data for a user.
