Administration

The top level page for administration section of the Alumni Archive is the Admin page. It has links to pages in the administrative section of the Alumni Archive website.

Which links are displayed there depends on your Alumni Archive role.

For most users, the Admin page has only the link to the Self Data page. That page is where you can update your registration data and change your password. See Self Data for more information.

If you are a PAO or Chief American PD Officer (abroad), or a DOS Domestic Office Director, then you are the Alumni Archive authorizing agent for any DOS users from your Country, or from your Office. Your Admin page will also include links to the pages needed to perform that function. See Administration for PAOs and Office Directors, for more information.
