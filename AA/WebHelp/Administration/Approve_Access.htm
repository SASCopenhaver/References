Approve Access page

The Approve Access page lists the DOS employees from your area who have registered with the Alumni Archive website and need to be verified/approved by you (as their authorizing agent) before they can be granted access.

Please process user registrations as soon as possible, because registrants cannot access the Archive until their request is approved.

You can click on one of the three option buttons on the right to Approve or Deny access, or you can put their registration On Hold until more information is obtained.

  1. While On Hold button, registrants cannot log on to the Archive. Their name will appear on your list on the Approve Changes page, where their status can to changed by using the Deny or Approve buttons.

  2. The Deny button will delete the user from the Archive registrants list. To gain access, they would need to re-register and be approved.

  3. The Approve button gives the user access to the Alumni Archive, at the level dictated by their registration data.

Clicking on a person's name will open the User Profile page, displaying the user registration data for that person.

If you have approved a person whom you now wish to put On Hold or Deny access to, go to the See Your Active Users page. There you can change any active user's status.
