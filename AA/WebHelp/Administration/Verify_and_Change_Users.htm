Verify and Change Users

The Verify and Change Users page lists and briefly describes lower level pages that you can use to administer users and registrations. Links to these pages are provided in the left column.

As a PAO or Chief American PD Officer (stationed abroad), or a DOS Domestic Office Director, you are the Alumni Archive authorizing agent for any potential users from your area. "Your area" means your Post abroad (the city, country, or countries for which you function as the principal PD Officer), or your DOS domestic Office. Therefore, you have access to the Requesting Access, Requesting Changes, and Active Users pages.

  • This means that you are the Officer responsible for granting or denying access to the Archive, for anyone from your area who has requested access by registering on the website.  "Your area" means your Post (and any other countries for which you function as the principal PD Officer), or your DOS Domestic Office.

  • You also need to approve any changes submitted by current user whose posting or role have changed, as their new situation needs to be verified and the appropriate access granted.

  • Normally, access is granted to any American Officers, and to American contractors and FSNs who are in a position to update the Archive's alumni records, or who need access to the Archive in order to generate alumni reports, contact lists, or mailing labels. This usually includes all PD personnel.

  • To process any waiting requests for access, open the Approve Access page by clicking on that link.

  • To process any waiting requests for changes, open the Approve Changes page by clicking on that link.

  • To look at registration data for all users in your area, or to rescind access for a current user, open the See Your Active Users page by clicking on that link.

  • See Administration for PAOs and Office Directors, for more details.
