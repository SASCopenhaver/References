Alumni Archive Help

Top of page

Help is available at three levels:

  1. This two-pane Help window, with topics covering the entire Alumni Archive website. This window includes a Table of Contents, an Index, a Search tab, and a brief Glossary. To open this Help window, click on the Help link at the top right of almost any page.

    • Use this level of Help when you are trying to find the right web page, or when you want to know more about a process or task, such as Updating Alumni Data.  

  2. There is also help for each webpage. This is directly accessible from each page by clicking on the page Help icon at the right of the page title. Use this type of Help when you just need some help with the page you are on.

  3. And there is help available at the control or field level. Click on the icon beside selected controls (that is, text boxes, list boxes, links, buttons, radio/option buttons, menus, or links); a small pop-up window will offer information about that control.

Within the Help topics, a blue link will take you to another Help topic, or to another part of the same topic page. You can use the browser Back button to return to your previous location.

A green link will open a new page with additional information (image or text). To return to the previous Help topic page:

  • Press the <Backspace> key, or

  • Press the <Alt>+<f> keys, or

  • Click on the Back link on the page, or

  • Use your browser's Back button.

 

If you find any bugs, or have any other problems with the Archive or the website, or if you have any suggestions or comments, please send them to the Archive Administrator, at AlumniArchive@state.gov.
