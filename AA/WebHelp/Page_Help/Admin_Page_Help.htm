Admin page

This is the front page for the administrative section of the Alumni Archive web site.

Which links are displayed there depends on your Alumni Archive role.

For most users, this page has only the link to the Self Data page. That page is where you can update your registration data and change your password.

If you are a PAO or Chief American PD Officer (abroad), or a DOS Domestic Office Director, then you are the Alumni Archive authorizing agent for any DOS users from your Post, or from your Office. Therefore, you also have a link to the Verify and Change Users page, which has further links to pages used to administer the access privileges of your users.
