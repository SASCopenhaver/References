Navigation Bar

The Global Navigation Bar (NavBar) is displayed at the top of most pages on this site. This makes it easy to move between different sections of the site without having to return to the Home page.

 

Each of the links on the bar is either a link to the main page for that section of the website, or a direct link to the initial page used for that function.

When you place the mouse cursor over some of these NavBar selections, they will display, below the NavBar, a set of links to some of the principal pages in that section. Click on one of the those links to open that page.
