Registration

The Alumni Archive is a restricted site; only registered State Department users have access to it. Therefore, prospective users of the Archive must first register before they can be granted access.

In the U.S., Office Directors must approve each individual's access. Abroad, the Public Affairs Officer (PAO) or Chief American PD Officer must approve each individual's access.

To register:

At the Log In page:

  1. Click on the new user registration link.

  2. This opens the first New User Registration page.

On the first registration page:

  1. Fill in your OpenNet+ email address, and click on the Submit button.

  2. If you have entered a valid OpenNet+ email address, the New User Registration form page will open.

On the second registration page

  1. Enter your name and phone number.

  2. Your email address is auto-filled, using the OpenNet address you entered on the previous registration page.

  3. Your Login Name is always the same as the user name portion of your OpenNet address, and is also auto-filled.

  4. Enter a Password that meets these requirements:

    • Includes at least 8 characters (letters, numbers, and symbols).

    • Includes at least one upper case letter, one lower case letter, and one number.

    • Never use a dictionary word or common phrase in any language.

    • Avoid any names, dates, or other information associated with yourself.

    • NEVER give out your password.

  5. In the Confirm Password box, type in the password again. If this does not match the first entry (in the Password text box), you will get an error message when you try to submit your registration. Because the password characters you type are not displayed, this confirms that you typed what you thought you typed.

  6. The Security Question and your Security Answer are used to confirm your identity, if you ever forget your password and need to have it e-mailed to you. Remember the answer you enter here.

  7. In the Role list box, select the role that best fits your current position.

    • When you make this selection, controls for 3, 4, or 5 additional fields will be displayed below this box. These depend on which role you selected.

    • If your role (item 7, above) is either PAO or Chief American PD Officer abroad or State Dept. Domestic Office Director, then you will have limited administrative responsibilities with regard to Archive users from your Country or Office.

  8. If you are stationed in the U.S., you will have a list box for selecting the DoS Office Code where you are assigned.

  9. If the role you select is PAO or Chief American PD Officer abroad or State Dept. Domestic Office Director, a date box is displayed for entering the estimated date of your final departure from your current position or post. Enter your best estimate of that date.

  10. Select your current Country of Assignment from the drop-down list.

    • This selection will determine which cities are available in the Post/City list box.

  11. Select your current Post/City of Assignment from the drop-down list. If your city is not on the drop-down list, enter it in the text box below this list box.

  12. If you are an FSN, you will see the Additional Countries list box. If you have responsibility for other countries in addition to the one in which you are posted, enter those in the Additional Countries box.

    • The selected countries will be displayed to the right of this list box.

    • To select more than one additional country, hold the <Ctrl> key down while clicking on each country.

  13. When finished entering all of your data, click on the Continue button at the bottom of the form, to proceed to the next registration page.

On the third registration page

  1. The New User Registration confirmation displays the information you have just entered, in a table. Double check this information.

  2. If any corrections or additions are needed, click on the Back button (at the bottom) to return to the New User Registration form page and make those changes.

  3. You can add comments in the Comments text box at the bottom of the table.

    • If you are an FSN and you need access to Alumni data from more than one country, explain why in this box. By default, FSNs receive access only to Alumni data for their country of assignment.

  4. When all data is entered and correct, click on the Submit button at the bottom of the page.

The fourth Registration page

  1. An Information page will open, confirming that your registration has been submitted, and informing you that you will be receiving an email.

    You already have your User Name and Password; save these, as they will not be included in the email.
     

After your registration data is submitted, it will be verified by the appropriate authorizing agent. This is either your PAO (for persons stationed abroad) or your Office Director (for persons stationed in the U.S.).

The exact level and type of access that you receive is based on your role, as well as your country of assignment.

You need to update your personal registration data whenever any of it changes. See Updating Your Registration Data for details.
