Forgot Password page

If you have forgotten your password, the Forgot Password page allows you to have your password sent to your OpenNet email address. Administrators will never see your password.

  1. Enter your OpenNet email address.

  2. Select the security question that you chose when you registered.

  3. Type in your answer to that question.

  4. Click on the  Submit Request button.

  5. If your answer here matches the answer that you submitted when you registered, your password will be sent to your OpenNet email address.

  6. If you cannot remember the answer, you will need to email the Archive administrator, at AlumniArchive@State.gov.
