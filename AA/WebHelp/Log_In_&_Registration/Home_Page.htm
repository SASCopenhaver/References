Alumni Archive Home Page

The Alumni Archive Home page opens immediately after you log in.

The left panel has News and Announcements concerning the Archive and Alumni.

The right panel has a box for each of the three main sections of the Alumni Archive web site, with a link to the front page for each of those sections:

  • Update Alumni Info -- opens that page, which is used to find the records for specific alumni, in order to update their records.

  • Create and Print -- opens that section page, with descriptions and links to pages for creating labels, alumni lists, and a variety of reports.

       Or, you can use the lower links in the box here, to go directly to the following pages:

    • Create Mailing Lists

    • Create Contact List

    • Create Statistical Reports

  • Admin -- this section page has a link to the page where you can update your own user registration data and change your password. Please update whenever your assignment, contact information, or other data changes; and change your password regularly.

    • From the main Admin page, Authorizing Agents (PAOs and Domestic Office Directors) can access the pages used to verify and authorize access for users in their area.

    • Or, they can use the go here to approve your users link, at the bottom of the Admin box, to go directly to the Verify and Change Users page.

    • See Administration for PAOs and Office Directors, for more information.

       

If you find any bugs, or have any other problems with the Archive or the website, or if you have any suggestions or comments, please send them to the Archive Administrator, at AlumniArchive@state.gov.
