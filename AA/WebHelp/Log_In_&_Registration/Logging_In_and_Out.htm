Logging In and Out

The Alumni Archive is a restricted site; only registered State Department users have access to it. Therefore, a user name and password are required.

  1. In the U.S., Office Directors must approve each individual's access. Abroad, the Public Affairs Officer (PAO) or Chief American PD Officer must approve each individual's access.

When you click on your desktop icon to open the site, the first page that appears is the Log In page.

If you have not yet registered, click on the registration link at the top of the Log In page, to go to the first New User Registration page. Starting there, follow the procedure to register.

  • See Registration for more details.

To log in:

  1. Enter your OpenNet User Name.

  2. Enter your Alumni Archive Password.

  3. Click on the Login button.

  4. After a successful login, the Log In page is replaced by the Alumni Archive Home page.

  5. If you have forgotten your password, click on the Forgot your password? link to go to the Forgot Password page. If you remember your answer to the security question, which you entered when you registered, you can have your password emailed to your OpenNet account.

To log out

Click on the Log Out link at the upper right of any page. This logs you out and returns you to the Log In page.

 

If you find any bugs or have any other problems with the Archive or the website, or if you have any suggestions or comments, please send them to the Archive Administrator, at AlumniArchive@state.gov.
