New User Registration - Confirmation page

The New User Registration confirmation page displays a table with the information you have just entered in the Registration Form.

  1. Check the information for completeness and correctness.

  2. If any corrections or additions are needed, click on the Back button to return to the registration form page and make those changes.

  3. You can add comments in the Comments text box at the bottom of the table.

    1. If you are an FSN and you need access to Alumni data from more than one country, explain why in this box. By default, FSNs receive access only to Alumni data for their country of assignment.

  4. When all data is entered and correct, click on the Submit button at the bottom of the page.

  5. An Information page will open, telling you that your registration has been submitted, and that you will be receiving an email.

  6. After you receive that email, notifying you of the approval of your registration, you can go to the Login page and log onto the website.
