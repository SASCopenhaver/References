Updating Your Registration Data

Every Alumni Archive user needs to ensure that their registration data is updated whenever it changes. And they need to change their password periodically, in accordance with State Department guidelines. To update your data:

  1. Open the Self Data page

    • On the Global Navigation Bar (the NavBar), place your mouse pointer on the Admin link. The Admin menu will be displayed beneath the NavBar. Click on the Self Data link.

    • Or, on the Admin page, click on the Self Data link on the left.

  2. The Self Data page will open, displaying your registration data.

    1. The form displayed here is the same as the one used when you registered. If you change your Role, the necessary field controls will be displayed, to allow you to enter the appropriate data.

  3. Update any of your data that has changed.

    • Please be sure that all contact data is correct.

  4. Change your password, if you have had it for 150 days or more (the maximum is 180 days).

  5. Click on the Submit button.

Note that if you change your role, your country or city of assignment, or your departure date, or if you add any additional countries, your access will be put on hold temporarily, just until the authorizing agent can validate the change. This is to ensure that you will be given the appropriate access that is needed for your new assignment or role.
